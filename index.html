Meet Shaunieigh

"I started We Do Content Co. because I believe every couple deserves to relive the magic of their day—not weeks later, but right away. Your love story deserves to be captured and shared instantly."

As a professional content creator who started working with well-known brands before transitioning to weddings, I bring a unique perspective to capturing your special moments.

I'm not here to replace your photographer or videographer—I work alongside them to focus on candid, behind-the-scenes footage: your besties hyping you up, those last-minute dress adjustments, and the dance floor chaos.

Based on the Sunshine Coast and Brisbane, but always ready to travel, I'm all about storytelling and creating memories that you receive almost instantly!
